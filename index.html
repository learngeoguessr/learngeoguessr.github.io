Welcome to LearnGeoguessr!

The goal of this site is to help you learn the meta and all the tricks to become the next GeoGuessr prodigy.

Even though there are a few sites out there that are quite useful for learning GeoGuessr,
I feel like they are very unorganized and a bit hard to browse,
and exactly that's the reason why this site was created.


Use the menu on the left side of the screen for easier navigation.
